Meeting and Communication Protocols

Our meeting and communication protocols for the Sidera I program are designed to ensure clear, consistent, and efficient collaboration across all teams. This includes regular touchpoint for AstraLux Program Management, Systems Engineering, Hardware, and Software to align on key deliverables and progress. The Meeting Protocol Matrix outlines the schedule for each meeting, including the type of meeting, participants, and its frequency, ensuring all stakeholders are kept informed and engaged throughout the program lifecycle.
